- **Recommended Action:** Create and promote expert-led content, clinical studies, and data-driven insights related to health and beauty, clearly attributing them to Boots. Structure this content to be easily discoverable and citable by AI models, establishing Boots as a trusted and explicit source of information.
- **Potential Impact:** Medium

- **Confidence:** Medium
